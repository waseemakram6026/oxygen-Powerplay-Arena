Arena Rules & Policies

Refund Policy

  • Bookings once confirmed are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • In case of heavy rain making play impossible, a Credit Note will be issued for a future slot. No cash refunds.

Footwear Policy

  • Only Flat Sole or Turf Shoes are allowed.
  • STRICTLY NO SPIKES or Studs. Players wearing spikes will not be allowed to enter the turf area to protect the surface.

General Conduct

  • No Smoking, Drinking, or Spitting inside the arena premises.
  • Management reserves the right to admission.
